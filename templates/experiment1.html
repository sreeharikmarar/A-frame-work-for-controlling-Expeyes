Experiment 1: Light up an LED connected to OD0



ExpEyes has two digital outputs pins (labelled OD0 and OD1 ).You can drive these pins HIGH(4 to 5V)and LOW (0V)


To Light ON : make OD0 & OD1 are HIGH by putting values(OD0= '1' & OD1= '1')


To Light OFF: make OD0 & OD1 are LOW by putting values (OD0= '0' & OD1= '0')
